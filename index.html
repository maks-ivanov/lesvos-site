Find Out More!

Embark on the trip of your lifetime!

ExVos is an activity-based scavenger hunt across the Greek island of Lesvos. Grab your friends, sign up, and download our app - we'll take care of everything else! You'll get a list of challenges, and your team will rack up points by completing fun tasks and submitting them in our app, so choose the ones that suit your style and dive in! The top teams will get prizes and bragging rights to boot.

Get Started!

Choose your challenge

Adventure

Conquer the island and your fears. Trekking, motorcycling, water sports, archery, climbing are only a few of the ways to experience the extreme.

Explore

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere. Try out scenic routes, historic ruins, beautiful waterfalls and exotic nature.

Discover

Get a taste of the culture and treat yourself to the finer things. Check out therapeutic spa, wine tasting, tango meetings, and art festivals.

Give back

Connect to the island through service. Volunteer at the soup kitchen, clean up the beach, show love to the dogs at the animal shelter - make your impact on the community!
